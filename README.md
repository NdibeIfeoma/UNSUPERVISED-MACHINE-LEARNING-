# UNSUPERVISED-MACHINE-LEARNING-
 M & B INSURANCE provideS personalized experiences and tailored recommendations to enhance customer satisfaction and drive loyalty.
 A comprehensive understanding of customer personalities and insurance behavior, to effectively target and engage with individual customers  is very necsessary for the business objective.  
 

# OBJECTIVES
The goal of this project is to perform Exploratory Analysis and a Customer Personality Analysis, this will aid you segment customers based on their
Insurance Profile behavior and information. 

- Customer segmentation to give recommendations like saving plans, loans, wealth management, etc on target customer groups.
- Unsupervised learning techniques like Dimensionality reduction and Clustering to identify groups of customers with similar behavior and characteristicS.
- Conduct a customer personality analysis, which will enable M & B Insurance to gain insights into customer behaviors, interests, and preferences.
- Segment customer base, and develop personalized marketing strategies, and experiences that align with the unique personalities of their customers.
